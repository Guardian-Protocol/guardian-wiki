Unstake

In order to unstake your vara you can go to the unstake section on the guardian dapp

Here you can come and unstake your vara that has been staked before,

This transaction does not need a gas fee

You need wait 7 days until you can withdraw your tokens

Here are a few things that is important to note:

  • You can't revert the unstake process

  • you will be able to unstake all of your gVara

  • The token value evolve his price in proportion of the staking quantity so the price it will variate

Architecture

In order to unstake your vara, when you make the unstake action that will not have gas cost, the guardian proxy will send the unbound vara transaction and at the same time the proxy will send the unstake message to the contract.

Your gVARA will be transferred from your wallet and sent to the smart contract that will retain the tokens until the unstake process end.
